Be the Voice
Every change that occurs is backed by an opinion, a thought, and a voice. A Model United Nations conference provides a platform for bringing out these voices. A Model United Nations conference is an academic simulation of the United Nations where students play the role of delegates representing different countries and attempt to solve real global issues.
